About Costa Rica

Costa Rica is a rugged, rainforested Central America country with coastlines on the Caribbean and Pacific. Its capital, San Jose, is home to cultural and biodiversity, roughly a quarter of Costa Rica is made up of protected jungle, teeming with wildlife including spider monkeys and quetzal birds.

Top Attractions

  • Manuel Antonio National Park
  • Monteverde Cloud Forest Reserve
  • Tamarindo
  • San Jose
